## Notes on sources exhausted this session

- **industrialscenery.blogspot.com** dedicated posts for Calumet, Stickney, and O'Brien WRPs each contained
  70-120 embedded images (mostly reposts of MWRD's Facebook "historical photo of the day" series, 1920s-1930s
  construction). These were NOT fully harvested in prior sessions — only a handful of images per plant had been
  pulled. This session parsed each post's raw HTML to extract every image URL + caption and selected a
  chronologically diverse, non-duplicate subset, prioritizing 1930-1959-era photos to fill that gap era.
- The blog has **no MWRD-specific label/tag** (checked sidebar); "water" label search surfaced only a few
  unrelated posts. Search via `site:industrialscenery.blogspot.com <plant name>` was the effective method, not
  browsing tag pages.

  pre-MWRD suburban predecessor plant absorbed into the O'Brien WRP collection system in 1942 — added to
  treatment-plants-suburban with both a construction photo (1924) and a 1938 ILHAP aerial.

  almost entirely of contemporary (2022) satellite screenshots and goat/sheep vegetation-clearing photos —

  were already in the archive from a prior session (1920-08-20, 1921-09-28, 1922-05-04, 1924-05-21,
